Patan Multiple Campus

Patan Multiple Campus is a renowned academic institution located in Lalitpur, Nepal. It was established in 2011 with the aim of providing quality education to students in the surrounding areas and beyond. The campus is affiliated with Tribhuvan University, which is the oldest and largest university in Nepal. The campus offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate degree programs in various fields such as science, humanities, management, and education. The academic programs are designed to provide students with a comprehensive education and prepare them for their future careers.

The campus is equipped with modern facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, computer labs, a library, a cafeteria, and sports facilities. The campus also has a team of experienced and qualified faculty members who are dedicated to providing high-quality education to their students.In addition to academic programs, Patan Multiple Campus also offers various extracurricular activities and events to enhance the overall development of its students. These include sports events, cultural programs, seminars, and workshops.

Overall, Patan Multiple Campus is a leading academic institution in Nepal that is committed to providing quality education to its students. It is an excellent choice for students who are looking for a well-rounded education that will prepare them for their future careers.
