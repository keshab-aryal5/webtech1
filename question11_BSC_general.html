BSC General

The Institute of Science and Technology, Tribhuvan University, provides a four-year annual bachelor's degree program in physics. This program focuses on the universe's physical qualities, from the tiniest subatomic particles to the largest remote galaxies. It is the study of time, matter, energy, space, atoms, and their interactions in order to determine the most fundamental principle of our universe's existence. Physics is essential for comprehending the origins of our planet, as well as the worlds beyond it.

Missions and Objectives

  • To demonstrate competency in understanding the most fundamental laws of physics
  • To display critical thinking abilities in order to pursue higher-level professions in research at academic institutions and other companies in order to apply physical principles to a broader range of applications.
  • To demonstrate the ability to use observations and experiments to test existing theories and quantitative models, refine and develop new models.
  • To prepare graduates to seek MSc and Doctoral degrees at leading research universities across the world.
  • Develop critical thinking skills, technical skills, and be equally adept at using the lab and research resources in a practical manner.
